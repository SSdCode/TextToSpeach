Welcome & Course Overview
"Welcome to this course on building and automating custom Android ROMs. Over the next few modules, you'll learn how to create your own customized Android system tailored for devices like the Redmi Note 7 Pro. Whether you're a beginner or have some programming experience, this course is designed to guide you step-by-step toward developing your own custom ROM, with hands-on examples and practical tips. So, let’s get started!"
